MAJOR CHEM PTY LTD was established with the vision of becoming a leading manufacturer of high quality cleaning and chemical productsin Botswana, and over the years , the company has steadily grown by consistently delivering reliable, effective, and affordable solutions to both individual and commercial clients.From its early beginnings as a small-scale operation, the company has expanded its product range and improved its production processes through continous innovation, allowing it to meet the evolving demands of the market while maintaining high standards of quality and safety.
